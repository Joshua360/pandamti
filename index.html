Welcome!

We are Pandamti, a nonprofit focused on reforestation of the Kenyan countryside. We are just a bunch of guys who want to plant some trees and conserve the environment.

Our main focus is the Kenyan countryside. We want to engage Kenyans in conserving their environment by planting trees.

Anyone who is interested in supporting our cause is free to sign up and join the fight to help save our environment.

Trees are Important

Trees Combat Climate Change

What is climate?Climate is the average weather of a place in many years.

What is climate change?Climate is a change in global or regional climate patterns especially a change evident from the mid to late 20th century onwards which is mainly attributed to increased levels of atmospheric carbon dioxide produced by the use of fossil fuels.

Problem: carbon dioxide (CO2) keeps building up in our atmosphere and this contributes to climate change.

Solution:Trees absorb carbon dioxide. They remove and store the carbon, at the same time releasing Oxygen back into the air.

Why Trees?

Problem: Excess carbon dioxide (CO2) keeps building up in our atmosphere and this contributes to climate change.

Solution: Trees absorb the carbon dioxide. They remove and store the carbon, at the same time releasing Oxygen back into the air.

Trees filter the air we breathe: Trees absorb odors and pollutant gases (nitrogen oxides, ammonia, sulfur dioxide and ozone) and filter particulates out of the air by trapping them on their leaves and bark.
